My Brief Biography


Eric Lui was born in Maryland and started playing go at age 5, becoming AGA 1 dan in less than a year. In 2010, he finished 2nd in the North American Ing Masters. In 2016, he became an AGA professional. To date, Eric has had more than twenty international representations for the US and North America to major tournaments including the Samsung Cup, MLILY Cup, Ing Cup, Chunlan Cup, Tianfu Cup, IMSA Elite Mind Games, Sankei Cup, Pair Go World Cup, World Student Go Oza, and five times to the World Youth Go Championship. He twice represented the US to the World Amateur Go Championship (2009, 2011) and Korea Prime Minister Cup International Baduk Championship (2006, 2015), achieving the best-ever US finish in both tournaments.
