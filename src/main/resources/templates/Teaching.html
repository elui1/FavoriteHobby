Everybody wants to get stronger. This is something that can't be helped, whether you are a serious student of the game, or just a casual player. Nowadays, there are so many resources out there available to us. Go books, YouTube videos, AI programs, you name it. And yet, improving is still a struggle for most of us. Why?


Improving at go requires time and focus, two things that are at a premium in today's world. Most Western players, even at strong amateur levels, are weak at fundamentals. For the most part this is inevitable, as the majority of players do not know the most efficient methods to improve.


I will give you some advice right now, totally free. The most important part of the game is the middle game. If your aim is to get stronger and win more games, then that's where you should focus most your efforts. This means playing games against players at your level and solving life-and-death problems.
