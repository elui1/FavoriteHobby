My name is Eric Lui. Since 2016, I am a professional Go player of the American Go Association (AGA), the main organizing body for Go in the USA.


If you're new to the game...

Welcome! Go is a game of unlimited beauty. The game has appeared in movies such as A Beautiful Mind, Tron: Legacy, and Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon. Some of the world's greatest minds, including Albert Einstein and John Nash, played Go. There are tens of millions of players worldwide, mostly in Asia, with numbers steadily increasing in the US.
